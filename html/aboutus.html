The idea was born in late August of 2020 inside the mind of our CEO and Crypto OG Julian Romero who, with the help of his wife and current COO of the company Maria de los Angeles Gonzalez developed the idea and established the company from scratch on early September of the same year with the incorporation of our CTO Jeisson Niño.
