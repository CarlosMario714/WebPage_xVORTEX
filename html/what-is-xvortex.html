What is xVORTEX?

Septiembre 10

In the midst of this new Crypto era, surrounded by all kinds of new and exciting projects that promise to open new and unexplored possibilities for the market itself and its participants, the focus most of the time tends to gravitate towards inside investment but, what about the outside? If we can invest our crypto in projects that improve the field by working from the inside of it, can we not also invest in the already established marked that runs almost every country and, in that way, improve the field from the outside while also making some money in the process? Those were the questions that gave birth to the unique and ambitious project called xVORTEX PROJECT.


The xVORTEX PROJECT is a DeFi protocol built on the Ethereum and Binance chain that aims to generate profit for all of its supporters using a unique mixed investment strategy supervised by a governability system. The idea was born in late August of 2020 inside the mind of our CEO and Crypto OG Julian Romero who, with the help of his wife and current COO of the company Maria de los Angeles Gonzalez developed the idea and established the company from scratch on early September of the same year with the incorporation of our CTO Jeisson Niño. Since then, our team has grown and now encompasses a considerable number of professional eager to see this project thrive.

COO - Maria de los Angeles Gonzalez (left) and CEO - Julian Romero (right)

This is but the tip of the iceberg, a broad stroke on the project’s goals, history and logistics but, if you are interested in knowing more about all of these topics, we invite you to read our whitepaper and to explore our medium page where you will find articles detailing a range of topics related to the company, from the status of the project and news about scheduling all the way to insight into our members Carrier, and a closer look to how the project was born.

If after reading all this information you want to be a part of the project then you can participate on the auction of our NFTs which will determine the members of our governance counsel or stake our token TRESs after the token sale.
