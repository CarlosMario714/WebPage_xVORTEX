Earn an attractive APY% on your New xVORTEX tokens by staking your tokens in one of our exclusive, secure staking pools below, in partnership with Ferrum Network.

How does it work?

Staking requires participants to lock tokens in a staking pool for a fixed period of time in return for highly competitive APY (Annual Percentage Yield) returns. Each pool offers different APY% rates, with the highest returns reserved for Liquidity Pool (LP) token providers who have added liquidity to either UniSwap (ETH) or PancakeSwap (BNB).

Please note all APY% figures displayed are annual rates of return, final staking rewards will be relative to the maturity period of each pool.
